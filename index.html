Our Philosophy

Why We Exist

Reclaiming pleasure, consent, and intimacy education for our community

Trauma-Informed

Every workshop is designed with psychological safety at its core. Opt-out is always an option, and consent is practiced, not just preached.

Community-Centered

We center Black voices, bodies, and experiences in spaces that have historically excluded us. Your identity is honoured here.

Academically Rigorous

Our curriculum draws from sexology, psychology, and somatic practices. We cite our sources and update our content annually.
